What the Poster Says

  • Question: Do R298K and V85A meaningfully alter HIV-1 Env?
  • Structural result: Near-identical global folding, with RMSD values of 0.33–0.37 Å and TM-scores of ~0.99.
  • Energetic result: Only subtle electrostatic differences were observed.
  • What was not shown: No major structural change. No proven functional enhancement. No proof of increased CCT.
  • Takeaway: The findings suggest subtle shifts worth exploring further, especially with other mutations and deeper analyses.
This project does not claim a strong significant effect from these two mutations alone. It supports a larger question about whether small biophysical differences could matter in HIV-1 Env behavior.
